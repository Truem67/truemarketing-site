Frequently asked questions

How do I subscribe?
Click the subscribe button on your chosen plan — it will open a pre-filled email. Send it and we'll activate your account within 1 hour during business hours.
Can I cancel at any time?
Yes. Email johan@doxseal.co.za with the subject "Cancel Subscription" and your access stops at the end of your current billing period. No penalties, no questions asked — as required by the Consumer Protection Act.
Do unused seals roll over?
No. Unused seals expire at the end of each monthly billing period.
What is the refund policy?
Because each seal involves an irreversible blockchain transaction with real network costs, we cannot refund fees for seals that have already been broadcast. If you cancel before using any seals in a billing period, contact us within 48 hours and we will review your request.
I'm still on my free trial — what happens when I subscribe?
Your existing account and certificate history are preserved. You keep the same login — no new password needed.

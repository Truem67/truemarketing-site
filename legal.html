2. Description of Service

DoxSeal provides a digital timestamping and document authentication service. When you "seal" a document or message, DoxSeal:

  • Computes a SHA-256 cryptographic hash of your content on your device;
  • Records that hash in an OP_RETURN transaction on the BSV blockchain;
  • Issues a verifiable Proof Certificate containing the transaction ID.

Important: DoxSeal provides timestamping and proof-of-existence only. We do not provide legal advice, and nothing on this platform constitutes legal advice. Admissibility of blockchain evidence in any legal proceeding — including CCMA arbitrations — depends on facts, context, and applicable law. We make no guarantee of admissibility.
